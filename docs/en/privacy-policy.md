# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 10, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to LUNIA ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and protect your information.

This Privacy Policy applies to our website at lunia.health (the "Website") and describes how we handle information collected through our early access registration form. This policy does NOT apply to the LUNIA health platform application, which is not yet available and will have separate privacy policies when launched.

By using our Website and submitting the early access form, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide to Us

When you complete our early access registration form, we collect:

- **Name**: Your first and last name
- **Email Address**: Your email address for communications
- **Interest Information**: Any optional message you choose to provide about your interest in LUNIA

### 2.2 Information Automatically Collected

When you visit our Website and submit the early access form, we automatically collect:

#### Device and Browser Information
- User agent (browser type and version)
- Screen resolution
- Browser language preferences
- Referrer URL (the website you came from)

#### Location Information
- IP address
- Approximate geographic location including:
  - City
  - Region/State
  - Country and country code
  - Timezone
  - Approximate latitude and longitude coordinates

#### Usage Information
- Date and time of form submission
- Pages visited on our Website

### 2.3 Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics (a Google service) to understand how visitors use our Website. Firebase Analytics may collect:

- Pages viewed and time spent on pages
- Browser and device information
- General location information
- Traffic sources and user flow

For more information about how Google processes data, please see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

### 3.1 Primary Purposes
- **Early Access Registration**: To add you to our waitlist and notify you when LUNIA becomes available
- **Communications**: To send you updates about LUNIA, including launch announcements and product updates
- **Customer Support**: To respond to your inquiries and provide assistance

### 3.2 Analytics and Improvement
- **Website Analytics**: To understand how visitors use our Website and improve user experience
- **Security**: To detect and prevent spam, fraud, and abuse
- **Geographic Planning**: To understand where our users are located to plan appropriate services and compliance requirements

### 3.3 Legal and Administrative
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect our rights and the rights of others
- To enforce our Terms of Service

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where We Store Your Data

Your information is stored using Google Firebase services, which are hosted on Google Cloud Platform servers. These servers may be located in various regions globally.

### 4.2 How We Protect Your Data

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including:

- **Input Sanitization**: All form inputs are sanitized to prevent injection attacks and XSS
- **Email Validation**: Email addresses are validated for proper format
- **Spam Detection**: Automated systems detect and block suspicious submissions
- **Rate Limiting**: Limits on form submissions to prevent abuse
- **Access Controls**: Administrative access to data is restricted to authorized team members only (hello@lunia.health and filipe.manso@bitait.pt)
- **Encryption**: Data transmitted to our servers uses HTTPS encryption
- **Honeypot Protection**: Hidden fields catch automated bot submissions

### 4.3 Data Retention

We retain your early access registration information until:
- You request deletion of your data, OR
- We launch the LUNIA platform and transition to our main application (at which point you'll have the option to create a full account or request data deletion), OR
- We determine that we will not proceed with the LUNIA platform

## 5. How We Share Your Information

### 5.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We share your information with the following service providers who help us operate our Website:

- **Google Firebase/Firestore**: For database storage and hosting
- **Google Analytics (Firebase Analytics)**: For website analytics
- **ipapi.co**: For IP address geolocation (this service receives your IP address)

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and may only use it to provide services to us.

### 5.2 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

### 5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to:
- Comply with legal processes
- Protect our rights, property, or safety
- Protect the rights, property, or safety of others
- Prevent fraud or abuse

## 6. Your Privacy Rights

### 6.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to:
- Request access to the personal information we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request a copy of your data in a portable format

### 6.2 Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal information at any time. To request deletion, email us at hello@lunia.health with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include the email address you used to register.

### 6.3 Marketing Communications

You may opt out of receiving marketing communications from us at any time by:
- Clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send you
- Emailing us at hello@lunia.health with the subject "Unsubscribe"

### 6.4 Do Not Track

Our Website does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" signals from browsers.

### 6.5 Regional Rights

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights:

**European Union (GDPR)**
- Right to data portability
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to object to processing
- Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

**California (CCPA/CPRA)**
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or shared
- Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

**Other Jurisdictions**
If you are located in a jurisdiction with specific privacy laws, you may have additional rights. Contact us to exercise your rights.

If you are accessing our Website from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from the laws of your country.

By using our Website, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States and other countries as necessary to provide our services.

Our Website is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly.

If you are under 18, please obtain your parent or guardian's permission before providing any personal information to us.
