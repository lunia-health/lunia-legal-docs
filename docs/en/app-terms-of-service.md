LUNIA is an empathetic AI companion application designed for women experiencing perimenopause and menopause. The Service provides:

- **Clinical Reports**: Generate structured reports to share with your healthcare provider, including symptom timelines and AI-suggested questions.
- **Community Forum**: Anonymous or identified participation in a moderated peer support community.
- **Educational Content**: Curated health information and self-care resources.
